User Registration and Login

Create an account and access the system by logging in. Registered users can access their profile, including their pet's information, and track their pet's health and well-being.

Pet Breed recommendations

Information about various pet breeds and their characteristics in order to assist you in selecting the best pet for your lifestyle, and also provide information on the necessary equipment and supplies for pet care.

3

Tracking and Record-Keeping

To allow pet owners to track their pet's health and wellness, including vet visits, medications, and vaccinations. You can also record their pet's daily activities, including feeding, exercise, and bathroom breaks.

4

Pet Health Record Keeper

To help you store your pet's health records in one place. You can record your pet's vaccination history, medical procedures, and any health conditions or medications they are currently taking. This feature can also include reminders for upcoming appointments and medications.

5

Matching

To match people with emotional support pets based on their needs and preferences. The system can use algorithms that take into account factors such as the person's living situation, the type of pet they want, and the pet's temperament.

6

Pet-Friendly Housing Search

To provide users with a search feature for finding pet-friendly housing options.
